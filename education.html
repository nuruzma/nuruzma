Lower level Education

For lower level of education, it is started from the elementary school.

The name of the school is Sekolah Kebangsaan Taman Permata located in Ulu Klang Kuala Lumpur

I started my shool education on the age of 7. I finished my school at the age of 12 and with the participation of UPSR exam.

The school leaves a lot of good memories of me and my friends and i hope I will get another chance to visit my old school again especially after the pandemic.



The next one is high school and the name of the school is Sekolah Menegnah Kebangsaan Seri Keramat

I started my high school at the age of 13 and finished at the age of 17 with the certificate of SPM result

During high school I learn alot about future career and gein more experience about life

It also guides my to be a better person in the phase of entering adulthood



Higher Level Education

For higher level of education, it is after i graduated high school

I continued my studies by filling up the application to UiTM Kampus Jengka Pahang for Pre-Diploma

I fulfilled my pre-diploma studies for 1 semester which is at the length of 6 months

Im feeling thankful because i get a good result which can make me continue my studies to diploma level

During being a student i get to experience a college student life and make many friends and gain more knowledge which I will forever be grateful for





After I finished my pre-diploma, I enter my application for UiTM Kampus Rembau Negeri Sembilan for course of Information Management

i succeed in entering as an IM student and now currently in the final year and finishing my studies as a diploma student

I really hope i get to do well in my studies and i will always try my best to finish my semester with a flying colors
